My Motivation Factor Towards Online Education

Many of life's failures are people who
did not realize how close they were to success
when they gave up.

- Thomas Edison -

I am keeping on to that qoute to further my passion for success in my life and not to
give up easily. I always remember my family's message to study hard so that I can achieve my goals in life.
So far, I have managed to pursue my degree and my intention is to continue studying until Master
InshaAllah. So, below are the factors that motivated me towards online education.

Can learn to manage time efficiently

Not wasting time
  • With online distance learning, I was able to manage my
    time between responsibilities at home and as a student.
    At first, I was not able to deal with online learning
    as it had never been before. I can divide my time to
    complete the assignments given by the lecturers.
    Besides that, I also can do house chores like
    tidying, cloth drapery and so on.
    		•
  • Online education is actually very good because it will save time.
    While online class, I can do while eating and will not stress if only
    focus on studying. Sometimes, some lecturers will provide video
    tutorials and exercises, then students will need to do the work
    and submit. During my free time, I would fill my time with the
    completion of pending works. This way, I can do
    assignments while studying.
    		•

    Always get information on time

    Stay positive!
  • The environment in the classroom and at home is very different.
    This is because when learning in the classroom, can ask questions
    during the class and before the end of office hours. When studying
    using online learning, if do not know how to answer and
    ask lecturers, it will always be informed on time. Just as
    students should always be alert with the phone because the
    lecturers will give more assignments.
    		•
  • When online learning, I felt overwhelmed and left behind because
    I did not understand what the lecturer explained. Although
    not easy for me to change my mind set to not think negative, I
    will try to think positive and also complete of all my assignments
    and attend a lecture. But now, I remain stay positive during
    the online learning because I realized when
    think positive everything will be easier.
